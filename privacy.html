QM Panel — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 18 July 2026

QM Panel ("the App") is an internal order-fulfillment and shop-management tool used by its operator and a small group of Etsy sellers who explicitly authorize it via Etsy OAuth. This policy explains what data the App accesses, how it is used, and how it is protected.

1. Who we are

The App is operated by an independent Etsy fulfillment provider. It is not a public consumer product; only the operator and sellers who grant OAuth consent use it. Contact: ejderusa@gmail.com.

2. Data we access

Only after a seller grants OAuth consent, and only through Etsy's official Open API v3, the App accesses:

  • Shop information (shop name, sales counts, follower counts)
  • Active listings (title, price, SKU, quantity, variations)
  • Order/receipt details required to fulfill an order: buyer name, shipping address, item title, SKU, quantity, variations, order totals, order status, and shipping/tracking information

The App does not receive buyer email addresses; Etsy does not provide them through the API, and we do not attempt to obtain them by other means.

3. How we use data

Order and shop data is used solely to fulfill orders — packaging and shipping — and to manage listings and inventory for the authorizing shops. We do not use buyer data for marketing, advertising, profiling, or any communication with buyers outside of Etsy.

4. Storage & security

OAuth access and refresh tokens are stored securely on our backend (Supabase) with row-level security and are never exposed to the browser or to any client. Order data is retrieved on demand for fulfillment and is not resold. Access to the backend is restricted to the App's server functions.

5. Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share seller or buyer data with third parties. Data is used only to provide the fulfillment service to the authorizing seller.

6. Retention & revocation

A seller may revoke the App's access at any time from their Etsy account settings (Account → Security → Apps). Upon revocation, the App stops accessing that seller's data and the stored tokens become invalid.

7. Compliance

The App operates in accordance with Etsy's API Terms of Use and applicable data-protection principles. Buyer personal data is processed only as necessary to fulfill the buyer's Etsy order.

8. Changes

We may update this policy as the App evolves. The "Last updated" date above reflects the latest revision.
